Germ Battle Game (Flash)

Try Germ Defence instead.

Stop the Germ Invasion! Use soap, iodine, antibodies, and fruit.
You can also slow them down by freezing.

The idea is to end up with a positive "Health Score". You can choose any Level. Try using different combinations of defenses in different locations. But be careful: the germs start replicating after a while, so best get them early!
