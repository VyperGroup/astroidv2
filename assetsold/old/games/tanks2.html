Tanks 2

Multiple terrains, multiple weapons - get them before they get you!

Adjust force and angle. Buy weapons and fuel. Destroy the other tank.

Controls


Use buttons or these keys:
• up/down for amount of force,
• left/right for angle
• a/d to move left/right (max 10 moves per turn)
